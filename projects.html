I've displayed a few of my projects on this website! These projects have been to help me learn different programming languages and concepts through a hands-on experience. I have also included school-related projects that I found impressive :)

While initially learning JavaScript, I decided to put what I had learnt to the test and create a simple Blackjack game. This was a way for me to really dive into the language and discover things for myself. It was also a chance for me to do some front-end design with CSS.

I decided to learn about Python's Selenium and see how hard it would be to automate Instagram browsing and activities. I documented the process in a tutorial series on YouTube which now has a few thousand views!
