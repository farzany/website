Location History Stats

Stats are always fun - especially when they're about your every-day life. Wondering to myself about how far I've travelled by bus since starting at McMaster, I decided to create a python program that would estimate information such as that. Taking easily-downloadable Google account data, this program sifts through numerous json files and tallies up the distances travelled in every seperate trip, and the corresponding means of travel. Read about how it works on Github!

Blackjack

While initially learning JavaScript, I decided to put what I had learnt to the test and create a simple Blackjack game. Starting a project like this was a way for me to really dive into the language and discover things for myself. It was also a chance for me to do some front-end design with CSS. The gameplay is slightly slowed down to create a semi-organic flow. I hope you enjoy my casual game!

Instagram Automation

I've always loved the idea of creating programs that would complete repetitive tasks for you. One of the first programs I actually made was a bot that played BlackJack for me in a Minecraft server! I decided to learn more about Selenium and see how hard it would be to automate Instagram browsing and activities. I documented the process in a tutorial series on YouTube which now has a few thousand views!
